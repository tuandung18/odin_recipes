Pho Bo

Ingredients:

  • 500g beef bones
  • 500g beef brisket, thinly sliced
  • 200g rice noodles
  • 1 onion, charred
  • 3-inch piece of ginger, charred
  • 3 star anise
  • 3 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Fresh herbs, bean sprouts, lime wedges, and sliced chili for serving

Steps:

  1. Char the onion and ginger over an open flame or in the oven until slightly blackened.
  2. In a large pot, bring beef bones and water to a boil. Drain and rinse the bones, then refill the pot with clean water.
  3. Add the charred onion, ginger, star anise, cloves, and cinnamon stick to the pot. Simmer for 1-2 hours, skimming any impurities that rise to the surface.
  4. Strain the broth and return it to the pot. Season with fish sauce and sugar.
  5. Boil the rice noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  6. Arrange the sliced beef brisket on a plate.
  7. In each serving bowl, place a portion of cooked rice noodles and raw beef slices.
  8. Ladle the hot broth over the beef to partially cook it.
  9. Serve the Phở Bò with fresh herbs, bean sprouts, lime wedges, and sliced chili on the side.
