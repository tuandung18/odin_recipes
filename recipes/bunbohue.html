Bun Bo Hue

Ingredients:

  • 500g beef shank, thinly sliced
  • 300g pork hock, boiled and sliced
  • 200g pork blood cubes
  • 150g rice vermicelli noodles
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon shrimp paste
  • 1 tablespoon chili oil
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Lime wedges, fresh herbs, and bean sprouts for serving

Steps:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the beef shank slices and sear until browned.
  3. Stir in the shrimp paste and chili oil, then add water to cover the meat.
  4. Bring the broth to a boil, then reduce to a simmer and cook for about 2 hours.
  5. Skim off any impurities that rise to the surface of the broth.
  6. Season the broth with fish sauce, sugar, and additional chili oil if desired.
  7. Boil the rice vermicelli noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  8. Arrange the cooked beef shank, pork hock, and pork blood cubes in serving bowls.
  9. Ladle the hot broth over the meats in each bowl.
  10. Serve the Bún Bò Huế with lime wedges, fresh herbs, and bean sprouts on the side.
