Bun Bo Hue

Ingredients:

  • 500g of flour
  • 10g of yeast
  • 300ml of warm water
  • 5g of salt

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for about 10 minutes.
  2. In a separate large bowl, mix the flour and salt together.
  3. Gradually add the dissolved yeast mixture to the flour mixture and knead until a soft, smooth dough forms.
  4. Cover the dough with a clean cloth and let it rise for 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.
  5. Turn the dough out onto a surface and gently deflate to remove excess air.
  6. Shape the dough into a loaf and let it rise for an additional 30 minutes.
  7. Before baking, score the surface of the loaf by making small cuts with a sharp knife.
  8. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C and bake the bread for about 25-30 minutes, or until the surface is golden brown and the bread is puffed up.
  9. Allow the bread to cool before enjoying.
